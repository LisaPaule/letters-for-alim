These are the reflection prompts I'll be using in my journal for Weeks 1-4. They're designed to help me heal, get clarity, and avoid repeating the same dynamics if we decided to try again:
Week 1: Raw Processing

What emotions am I feeling right now?

What specific moments in the relationship am I replaying? Why those?

What was already not working months before the breakup?

What do I fear most about being without him?

Goal:

What do I miss most without him?

Week 2: Self-Awareness

In fights, what role did I usually play (e.g. defensive, critical, avoidant, anxious)?

When I felt hurt, how did I usually express it? Did it make things better or worse?

What unmet needs did I have that I never clearly communicated?

What unmet needs did he have I may have overlooked or underestimated?

Week 3: Relationship Dynamics

Which fights were about real issues, and which were about stress/long distance?

Did I listen to him as much as I wanted him to listen to me?

What behaviours of mine might have made him feel pressured, misunderstood or unappreciated?

If I could redo one fight, how would I handle it differently?

Week 4: Future Vision

If he's open to talking -> suggest a call or meeting. Keep the conversation focused on the future, not rehashing fights.

If we tried again, what concrete changes would I commit to making?

What boundaries would I need to set for my own emotional health?

What would I need from him to feel secure and loved again?

If he never comes back, what can I learn from this relationship that makes me stronger for the next one?
