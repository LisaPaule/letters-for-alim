Love letters for my love

These letters are for any possible scenario in our relationship like when I'm not there to tell you, or when you need to feel loved and don't want to ask for it. But also when we're in places we can't imagine right now. This is for when you need to understand what's running through my mind, but can't or don't want to ask. This is for when you need to be reminded of your girlfriend and how she thought (since she doesn't exist in that way anymore.)

Open this when...

...when I'm not there to tell you, and you have feelings you need validation for or thoughts you need to feel heard, I want you to open these letters and be reminded that there is a person out there who loves you, always, forever and deeply.

I didn't write a letter for this because it's two simple sentences: If by any chance you don't believe in yourself anymore, borrow my belief in you, until you can believe in yourself again. And if by any chance you don't trust in yourself and the choices you make, borrow my belief in that you will always do what's best for yourself and your surrounding.

And if by any chance, there comes a day in which you don't believe these words no more, in which we've drifted apart, remember: I wrote these letters out of love for you, not out of trust, not out of passion, not out of respect. These are a compilation of true love for the person you are. And I will never stop loving you. I haven't now either. You will always have a chunk of my heart, even when I loose my spot in yours. And as long as you live, as long as you stay true to yourself and your beliefs, these letters will have meaning the same way they did when I wrote them.

To love you has been and always will be the pleasure and peace in my life. You are mine and I am yours.

Anniversary Letters

Every month of every year and every year for the rest of our lives, I hope I get to write such letters to you. Letters that compile not only my love, but also my vision, my view, my feelings and my hope for us. These letters are supposed to be a constant reminder that we have gone through so much and we survived, as individuals and as a couple. They are supposed to show you, that we can. We can be that exeption. We can love and we can make it work. They're supposed to be reflections, and if they work, we will sit in a couple years and read these and laugh at all the moments captured, reminisce about all the times shared. They are reminders of who me and you are.

Break Up Journal

This is the hardest section of them all. It's the section that discusses the unsaid issues that came within the relationship and led to the end of it. It's the part that shows the aspects we didn't handle well, and is my way of holding space for you. It's my hope for a future and my capturing of a time in which we were apart, with the hope for reflecting and showing my personal progress through our time apart. This is the section that holds the most potential and the one hardest do describe as anything other than painful. It's my call to you that says "Stay with me.", but also my admission in that I trust you. I trust you in that you always do what's best for yourself and us. And if you think this is the only way we have a chance, then I will trust. This is the proof for my unyielding love and trust for and in you.
