September 9th

Day 14 Post-Break Up

It's funny how I was so convinced it would be incredibly hard to move on from you, but it took me one fun time out drinking and a healthy routine to feel happy again. One thing I feel I need to mention is that throughout all of this–the talks with Adelina, the tears shed over you and the fights with my family over whether there was a bet, whether I'm a slut or not, and whatever other conversation I was forced to indulge in because of you–I never lied. I never defamed our relationship and I'm more aware of the ups and the downs we had. I didn't read through our chats because I know it wouldn't have been healthy. I left our shared folder because I knew one day you'd do it first and I didn't want to wait for that day. I'm stopping to chase you.

But you know what's funny? Throughout it, I continue thinking: He never said he doesn't love me anymore. You never said you fell out of love or that there was no future. You seemed hurt when I said there was no chance in the future. And I mean that. I've thought long and hard and I've come to the realisation we're entirely different in our communication. So unless you are willing and able to give me a full rundown of the events that led to this, I don't see how the trust could ever be restored. And maybe that's the point. Maybe it shouldn't. Maybe we both deserve different–not better, because we were quite perfect.

I'm starting to willingly forget you and I welcome the peace that comes with that. I'm starting to forgive myself for letting you walk away, and I welcome that because when I rationalise it, I realise time and time again that I held onto you so hard that my fingers bled.

I'll always miss you. But I also feel like I'll always feel betrayed. I doubt you ever think about the damage that you did, but I hold on to every detail like my life depends on it. I hear your voice every time that I think I'm not enough. How could anybody do the things you did so easily? It takes strength to forgive, but I don't feel strong. I sometimes fantasise about a time you're a little fucking sorry. And I try to understand why you would do this to me. You must be insecure, you must be so unhappy. And I know in my heart that hurt people hurt people. And we both drew blood, but man, those cuts were never equal.
