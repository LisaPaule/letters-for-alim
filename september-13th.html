Day 18 Post-Break Up

It's funny I still write to you here hahaha. I know I won't reach out to you again, but I also want you to be able to be part of my life if you want to be (somehow idk). I wish we had been able to be friends, but my love for you and your plan to hurt me made it impossible.

I hope one day we get the chance to sit down and we can talk. I hope one day you miss me enough to wish to try again, and I hope when that day comes, I love you again. I'm falling out of it. I'm talking with other people now. They're fun. They pick me up. They care and they show it to me in ways different to you. I look for you in them, but I don't find you.

I wish Mila had never entered your life or our relationship because then we'd still have a chance. But now, even if she might have been nothing, even if she was just a rebound or you never fell for her, you still chose her. You choose to love her and you left me when I needed you most.

I wonder whether we're in No Contact or whether we're just done. I wonder whether you've ever went on this website again. I wonder whether you ever will. I wonder what'll happen if you do. I wonder whether you've noticed I left our shared folder. I wonder whether you have me blocked on WhatsApp. I know you didn't block my number, but don't worry. I won't reach out.

I don't know how long I have left with these feelings inside of me. It's not because of you. It's barely because of me even. But there's just this emptiness I wake up and fall asleep with, and even when I try and sometimes manage to fill it during the day, it just never goes away.

I don't see a future anymore. I don't know why I'm writing this here, as physical proof, why I give you this power over me. It's not to guilt trip you. It's not to bring you back. You're just the only person I ever truly wanted. The only person I wanted to have in my life. Maybe that hurts too.
